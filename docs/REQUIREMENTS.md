# REQUIREMENTS.md

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

1. **User Onboarding**
	* FR-1: The platform shall provide a guided onboarding process for new users, including a tutorial and a series of interactive challenges to help them get familiar with the platform.
	* FR-2: The onboarding process shall be accessible on both desktop and mobile devices.
2. **Project Creation**
	* FR-3: The platform shall allow users to create new projects with a single click, without requiring any technical knowledge.
	* FR-4: The platform shall provide a library of pre-built templates and examples to help users get started with their projects.
3. **Visual Development**
	* FR-5: The platform shall provide a drag-and-drop interface for users to design and build their software products without writing code.
	* FR-6: The platform shall support a variety of visual components, including UI elements, APIs, and integrations with third-party services.
4. **Logic and Business Rules**
	* FR-7: The platform shall allow users to define logic and business rules for their software products using a visual interface.
	* FR-8: The platform shall support conditional statements, loops, and other basic programming constructs.
5. **Data Management**
	* FR-9: The platform shall provide a data management system that allows users to store and retrieve data from their software products.
	* FR-10: The platform shall support data validation, data types, and data relationships.
6. **Deployment and Publishing**
	* FR-11: The platform shall allow users to deploy their software products to various environments, including development, testing, and production.
	* FR-12: The platform shall provide a publishing feature that allows users to share their software products with others.

### Non-Functional Requirements

1. **Performance**
	* NFR-1: The platform shall respond to user input within 2 seconds.
	* NFR-2: The platform shall handle a minimum of 100 concurrent users without significant performance degradation.
2. **Security**
	* NFR-3: The platform shall implement robust authentication and authorization mechanisms to ensure that only authorized users can access and modify software products.
	* NFR-4: The platform shall encrypt all data stored and transmitted within the platform.
3. **Reliability**
	* NFR-5: The platform shall have a minimum uptime of 99.9% per month.
	* NFR-6: The platform shall provide regular backups and disaster recovery procedures to ensure business continuity.

### Constraints

1. **Technical Debt**
	* The platform shall not introduce any significant technical debt that would hinder future development or maintenance.
2. **Scalability**
	* The platform shall be designed to scale horizontally to handle increasing user demand.
3. **Integration**
	* The platform shall provide APIs and SDKs for integrating with third-party services and tools.

### Assumptions

1. **User Behavior**
	* Users shall be willing to learn and adapt to the platform's interface and features.
2. **Technical Expertise**
	* Users shall have a basic understanding of software development concepts and principles.
3. **Internet Connectivity**
	* Users shall have reliable internet connectivity to access the platform and its features.
