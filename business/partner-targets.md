# Partner Targets
## Overview
As a no-code/low-code development platform, creator-craft aims to integrate with various SaaS and APIs to expand its capabilities and user base. The following partner targets are identified based on their relevance to the creator economy, affiliate/revenue-share opportunities, and potential value-add to the platform.

## Partner Targets List

### 1. **GitHub**
* SaaS: GitHub
* Integration Effort: M
* Value-Add: Automate code deployment and version control for creators
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (GitHub Affiliate Program)
* Free-Tier Limits: 3 public repositories, unlimited private repositories (with paid plan)
* Rationale: GitHub is a popular platform for developers and creators. Integrating with GitHub will enable creators to deploy and manage their projects seamlessly.

### 2. **Figma**
* SaaS: Figma
* Integration Effort: M
* Value-Add: Enable designers to create and integrate UI/UX designs directly into creator-craft projects
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (Figma Affiliate Program)
* Free-Tier Limits: 3 projects, 2 editors, and 30 days of file history
* Rationale: Figma is a leading design platform. Integrating with Figma will allow creators to design and develop their projects in a unified workflow.

### 3. **Mailchimp**
* SaaS: Mailchimp
* Integration Effort: S
* Value-Add: Automate email marketing and user engagement for creators
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (Mailchimp Affiliate Program)
* Free-Tier Limits: 2,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails per month
* Rationale: Mailchimp is a popular email marketing platform. Integrating with Mailchimp will enable creators to manage their email lists and campaigns efficiently.

### 4. **Stripe**
* SaaS: Stripe
* Integration Effort: M
* Value-Add: Enable creators to manage payments and subscriptions directly within creator-craft
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (Stripe Affiliate Program)
* Free-Tier Limits: 1,000 transactions per month, 1% + 30¢ per transaction
* Rationale: Stripe is a leading payment gateway. Integrating with Stripe will allow creators to manage their financial transactions and subscriptions seamlessly.

### 5. **Notion**
* SaaS: Notion
* Integration Effort: S
* Value-Add: Enable creators to manage their projects, notes, and databases within creator-craft
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (Notion Affiliate Program)
* Free-Tier Limits: 100 pages, 100 blocks, and 1 user
* Rationale: Notion is a versatile note-taking and project management platform. Integrating with Notion will allow creators to manage their projects and data efficiently.

### 6. **Discord**
* SaaS: Discord
* Integration Effort: S
* Value-Add: Enable creators to manage their communities and engage with users directly within creator-craft
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (Discord Affiliate Program)
* Free-Tier Limits: 5 servers, 50 users, and 20 minutes of voice chat
* Rationale: Discord is a popular community platform. Integrating with Discord will enable creators to manage their communities and engage with users efficiently.

### 7. **Google Drive**
* SaaS: Google Drive
* Integration Effort: S
* Value-Add: Enable creators to manage their files and collaborate with team members directly within creator-craft
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: No
* Free-Tier Limits: 15 GB of storage
* Rationale: Google Drive is a popular cloud storage platform. Integrating with Google Drive will allow creators to manage their files and collaborate with team members efficiently.

### 8. **Twitch**
* SaaS: Twitch
* Integration Effort: M
* Value-Add: Enable creators to manage their live streams and engage with users directly within creator-craft
* Affiliate/Revenue-Share: Yes (Twitch Affiliate Program)
* Free-Tier Limits: 1 channel, 1 stream key, and 1,000 viewers
* Rationale: Twitch is a leading live streaming platform. Integrating with Twitch will enable creators to manage their live streams and engage with users efficiently.

## Next Steps
Prioritize the integration of GitHub, Figma, and Stripe, as they offer affiliate/revenue-share opportunities and have a significant impact on the creator economy. Develop a detailed integration roadmap and timeline for each partner target, considering the integration effort, value-add, and free-tier limits.
