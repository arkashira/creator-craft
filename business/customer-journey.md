# Customer Journey Map: Creator-Craft

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Indie creator sees social media post, forum discussion, or referral from fellow creator
- **Friction Points**: 
  - Overwhelming number of no-code tools available
  - Difficulty distinguishing Creator-Craft from competitors
  - Lack of clear value proposition without technical background
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity, skepticism, overwhelm
- **Opportunities to Delight**:
  - Viral demo video showcasing rapid prototyping
  - Testimonial from well-known indie creator
  - Clear differentiation in creator-focused features
- **Metric**: Awareness lift (social engagement, website traffic from organic sources)

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Creator actively searches for no-code solutions or attends relevant meetup
- **Friction Points**:
  - Complex pricing structure
  - Uncertainty about feature set vs. competition
  - Concerns about scalability for future growth
- **User Emotions**: Evaluation, hesitation, excitement
- **Opportunities to Delight**:
  - Free tier with robust core features
  - Interactive demo with guided walkthrough
  - Case studies showing real creator success stories
- **Metric**: Time spent on product pages, conversion rate from awareness to consideration

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Creator signs up for free trial or starts using basic plan
- **Friction Points**:
  - Learning curve despite "no-code" promise
  - Limited support during initial setup
  - Feature gaps discovered during first project
- **User Emotions**: Anticipation, frustration, discovery
- **Opportunities to Delight**:
  - Onboarding tutorial with progress tracking
  - Quick-start templates tailored to creator needs
  - Proactive email support for common issues
- **Metric**: Trial activation rate, time to first successful build, feature adoption rate

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Creator successfully launches their first product or reaches key milestone
- **Friction Points**:
  - Transitioning from free to paid plan
  - Need for additional features not included in basic package
  - Concerns about long-term viability of tool
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, confidence, commitment
- **Opportunities to Delight**:
  - Celebration of first launch with personalized message
  - Exclusive access to beta features or premium templates
  - Creator community integration and networking events
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid, retention rate after 30 days

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Creator achieves significant success with current product or wants to scale
- **Friction Points**:
  - Budget constraints for advanced features
  - Complexity of adding integrations or customizations
  - Need for more sophisticated analytics or monetization tools
- **User Emotions**: Growth, ambition, potential concern about tool limitations
- **Opportunities to Delight**:
  - Tiered pricing based on usage or revenue generated
  - Creator success stories highlighting expansion capabilities
  - Dedicated account manager for high-value creators
- **Metric**: Revenue per user, upsell conversion rate, net promoter score (NPS)
