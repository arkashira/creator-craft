# Marketing Plan

## Positioning
**Primary:** The fastest way for indie creators to turn ideas into software products without coding.
**Alternatives:**
1. Build and launch software products in hours, not months.
2. No-code platform for creators who want to own their software.
3. Turn your creative vision into a working product instantly.

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
1. **Alex the Content Creator**
   - **Daily Job:** Creates YouTube videos and manages a Patreon community.
   - **Budget:** $500/month for tools that help monetize content.
2. **Jamie the Indie Game Developer**
   - **Daily Job:** Designs and develops small indie games.
   - **Budget:** $1,000/month for tools that streamline development.
3. **Taylor the Digital Artist**
   - **Daily Job:** Creates and sells digital art on Etsy and Instagram.
   - **Budget:** $300/month for tools that help sell and manage art.

## Channels
1. **Social Media (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** High engagement with creators and visual content.
2. **YouTube Tutorials and Webinars**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Detailed tutorials attract creators looking to learn.
3. **Partnerships with Creator Communities (Patreon, Substack, Ko-fi)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $15 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Direct access to indie creators with specific needs.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Teaser content (screenshots, behind-the-scenes, testimonials).
- **Week 5-8:** Tutorials and how-to videos.
- **Week 9-12:** Case studies and success stories.
- **Week 13-16:** User-generated content and community highlights.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize beta testing with a small group of creators.
- **D-0:** Official launch with a live webinar and social media blitz.
- **D+30:** First major update based on user feedback.
- **D+90:** Expand partnerships and introduce premium features.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** 500 by D+90.
- **$MRR Target by D+90:** $10,000.
